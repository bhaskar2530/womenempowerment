Women's Rights in India

In India, women have certain fundamental rights that are protected by the Constitution. Some key rights include:

  • Right to Equality
  • Right to Freedom from Discrimination
  • Right to Life and Personal Liberty

It is essential to be aware of and promote women's rights to ensure a just and equal society.
